<p><p>The veterinary diagnostic kits market comprises several types, including antigen-antibody tests, PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction), and ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), among others. Antigen-antibody tests detect specific proteins or antibodies, providing rapid results for infectious diseases. PCR amplifies genetic material, allowing for precise detection of pathogens. ELISA quantifies antigens or antibodies, serving as a reliable tool for diagnosing various conditions. Additional methods may include serological tests and molecular diagnostics, each catering to specific veterinary diagnostic needs.</p></p>
