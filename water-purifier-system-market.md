<p><p>The water purifier system market includes various technologies such as activated carbon filters, UV technology, and reverse osmosis. Activated carbon filters effectively remove impurities and chlorine, enhancing water taste and odor. UV technology uses ultraviolet light to disinfect water, eliminating bacteria and viruses without chemical additives. Reverse osmosis involves pushing water through a semi-permeable membrane, effectively removing dissolved solids and contaminants. Each of these systems addresses different purification needs, catering to diverse consumer preferences and water quality concerns.</p></p>
