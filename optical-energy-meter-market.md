<p><p>The optical energy meter market comprises several types, including pyroelectric and photodiode sensors, among others. Pyroelectric sensors detect changes in temperature caused by incoming radiation, providing high sensitivity for measuring short pulses of energy. Photodiodes convert light into electrical current, offering rapid response times and wide spectral range for precise energy measurements. Other types may include thermopile sensors and bolometers, each designed for specific applications in research, industrial monitoring, and environmental studies, highlighting the diverse needs in the optical energy measurement sector.</p></p>
