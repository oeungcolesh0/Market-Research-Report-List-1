<p><p>The wireless charging system market primarily comprises two types of integrated circuits (ICs): transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs manage the power transfer, converting AC to DC and controlling the charging process for devices. On the other hand, receiver ICs are embedded within the device being charged, capturing the energy transmitted and converting it back into usable power for the device's battery. Together, these ICs ensure efficient and safe wireless charging, driving innovation in various consumer electronics.</p></p>
