<p><p>Eddy current proximity sensors are crucial in various industrial applications, classified into two primary market types: split type and integrated type. Split type eddy current sensors consist of a separate probe and electronics, allowing for flexible positioning and easier replacement of components. In contrast, integrated eddy current sensors feature the sensing element and electronics housed together, promoting compactness and simplified installation. Both types offer reliable non-contact measurement of metallic objects, enhancing precision in automation processes and machinery monitoring.</p></p>
