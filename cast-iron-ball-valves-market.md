<p><p>The Cast Iron Ball Valves market is primarily segmented into two types: Screwed Cast Iron Ball Valves and Flanged Cast Iron Ball Valves. Screwed Cast Iron Ball Valves feature threaded ends, allowing easy installation and disassembly, making them suitable for smaller pipelines. In contrast, Flanged Cast Iron Ball Valves come with flange connections, providing robust sealing and are ideal for larger and high-pressure applications. Both types are widely used in various industries due to their durability and effective flow control capabilities.</p></p>
