<p><p>The boron market can be categorized into two primary sources: Salt Lake Source and Mine Source. Salt Lake Source boron is derived from brine, where boron is extracted through evaporation processes from salty water bodies, primarily in regions like the Salar de Uyuni. Mine Source, on the other hand, involves the extraction of boron minerals, such as borax, from underground deposits. Both sources contribute to the overall supply, but their extraction methods and purity levels can vary significantly, impacting market dynamics.</p></p>
