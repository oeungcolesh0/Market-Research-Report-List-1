<p><p>The vacuum pump carbon vanes market comprises three main types: Carbon Graphite, Metal Impregnated Carbon Graphite, and Resin Bonded Graphite. Carbon Graphite vanes offer excellent thermal conductivity and lubrication properties, making them ideal for various vacuum applications. Metal Impregnated Carbon Graphite vanes enhance strength and wear resistance by incorporating metals, thus extending lifespan under high-stress conditions. Resin Bonded Graphite vanes use a resin binder, providing improved mechanical stability and reduced wear, making them suitable for demanding environments in vacuum technology.</p></p>
