<p><p>Self-compacting concrete (SCC) is a highly fluid concrete that can flow and consolidate under its own weight, eliminating the need for vibration. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Powder Type SCC** utilizes fine particles for enhanced stability and segregation resistance.</p><p>2. **Viscosity Agent Type SCC** incorporates additives that increase viscosity, improving flow without segregation.</p><p>3. **Combination Type SCC** integrates both powders and viscosity agents to achieve optimal performance for various applications, offering flexibility in construction processes.</p></p>
