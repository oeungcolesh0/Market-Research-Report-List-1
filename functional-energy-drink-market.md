<p><strong>The Functional Energy Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic</li><li>Hypertonic</li><li>Hypotonic</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The functional energy drink market is categorized into isotonic, hypertonic, and hypotonic drinks. Isotonic drinks, which contain similar osmotic pressure as body fluids, are designed for rapid hydration and energy replenishment during workouts. Hypertonic drinks, with higher sugar and electrolyte concentrations, provide sustained energy but require additional water for hydration. Hypotonic drinks, having lower osmotic pressure, are ideal for quick hydration with less energy content. There are also "other" beverages, including those targeting specific health needs or incorporating unique ingredients.</p></p>
