<p><p>The optical pulse and heart rate sensor market is experiencing significant growth, driven by advancements in healthcare technology and increasing health awareness. The market is divided into two main types: transmission and reflection sensors. Transmission sensors utilize light to pass through a medium, measuring the absorption variation caused by blood flow, while reflection sensors measure the light reflected off the skin's surface. Together, these technologies enhance heart rate monitoring accuracy and efficiency, catering to a diverse range of consumer and medical applications.</p></p>
