<p><ul><li>Chopper Type DC Voltmeters</li><li>Direct Coupled Type DC Voltmeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC voltmeters market can be broadly categorized into chopper type and direct coupled type DC voltmeters. Chopper type DC voltmeters utilize an electronic switching mechanism, allowing for high accuracy and stability in measurements, making them suitable for low voltage applications. Conversely, direct coupled type DC voltmeters offer a straightforward design that directly measures voltage without additional circuitry, providing simplicity and ease of use. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries, ensuring precise voltage measurement in various settings.</p></p>
