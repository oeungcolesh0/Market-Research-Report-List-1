<p><p>Asphalt anti-stripping agents are essential additives that enhance the durability of asphalt mixtures by preventing moisture-induced damage. The market is primarily divided into two types: amine anti-stripping agents, which use amine compounds to improve adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, ensuring better performance under wet conditions; and amine-free anti-stripping agents, which utilize alternative chemistry to achieve similar benefits without the use of amines, often providing more environmentally friendly options. Both types contribute to longer-lasting, high-quality asphalt pavements.</p></p>
