<p><p>The spectrometer market is categorized into atomic and molecular spectrometers. Atomic spectrometers focus on analyzing the elemental composition of samples by measuring the light emitted or absorbed by atoms, commonly utilized in fields like environmental monitoring and metallurgy. In contrast, molecular spectrometers study the molecular structure and dynamics of compounds through techniques like infrared and nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy. Both types are essential in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and materials science, providing valuable insights for quality control and research.</p></p>
