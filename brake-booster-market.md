<p><ul><li>Vacuum Booster</li><li>Hydraulic Booster</li><li>Air Pressure Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake booster market is primarily segmented into three types: vacuum boosters, hydraulic boosters, and air pressure boosters. Vacuum boosters use engine vacuum to amplify brake force, making them common in traditional vehicles. Hydraulic boosters leverage hydraulic pressure, often found in heavier vehicles for enhanced braking performance. Air pressure boosters utilize compressed air to assist braking, typically seen in commercial and large vehicles. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, response time, and application suitability.</p></p>
