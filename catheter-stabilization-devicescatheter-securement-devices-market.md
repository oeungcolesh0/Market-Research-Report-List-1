<p><p>The catheter stabilization devices market encompasses various types designed to secure catheters in place, enhancing patient safety and comfort. Arterial securement devices are specifically used for arterial catheters, ensuring consistent blood flow monitoring. Central venous catheter securement devices stabilize central lines, preventing displacement. Peripheral securement devices are aimed at peripheral IVs, reducing risk of infiltration and phlebitis. Finally, urinary securement devices keep urinary catheters anchored, minimizing accidental removal and improving patient mobility. Each type addresses unique clinical needs.</p></p>
