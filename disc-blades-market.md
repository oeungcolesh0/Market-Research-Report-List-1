<p><ul><li>Flat Disc Blades</li><li>Concave Disc Blades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disc blades market is divided into two main types: flat disc blades and concave disc blades. Flat disc blades are primarily used for cutting and tilling in agricultural practices, providing a smooth surface for efficient soil penetration. In contrast, concave disc blades are designed to create furrows and control soil displacement, making them ideal for contour farming and seedbed preparation. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing agricultural productivity and soil management.</p></p>
