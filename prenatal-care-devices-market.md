<p><p>The prenatal care devices market encompasses various technologies designed to monitor fetal health and development. Ultrasound and ultrasonography devices use sound waves to create imaging of the fetus, allowing for real-time assessments. Fetal Dopplers enable clinicians to detect the fetal heartbeat, assessing well-being efficiently. Fetal magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) offers detailed imaging for complex cases, while fetal monitors continuously track the baby's heart rate and uterine contractions during labor, ensuring timely interventions for maternal and fetal safety.</p></p>
