<p><p>Grain handling systems encompass various types essential for the efficient movement and storage of grain. Bucket elevators are vertical conveyors that transport grain upwards, optimizing storage space. Conveyors, often belt or chain-based, facilitate horizontal or inclined grain movement across facilities. Augers use a rotating screw mechanism to move grain through tubes, offering flexibility in loading and unloading. Other systems may include pneumatic conveyors and gravity-based methods, all designed to enhance grain handling efficiency and minimize loss during processing and storage.</p></p>
