<p><strong>The Automatic Vehicle Barrier Gate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Barrier Gate market comprises two main types: straight and crank barriers. Straight barriers feature a horizontal arm that swings straight up or down, providing quick access control for parking lots and toll booths. Crank barriers, on the other hand, have a pivoting mechanism allowing the arm to rise at an angle, often used in areas with space constraints. Both types enhance security and streamline vehicle flow in various applications, including residential complexes and commercial properties.</p></p>
