<p><ul><li>Medical Mechanical Scale</li><li>Medical Electronic Scale</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional medical scale market is primarily categorized into two types: medical mechanical scales and medical electronic scales. Medical mechanical scales operate on a balance system, relying on springs and levers to measure weight without the need for electricity, making them durable and portable. In contrast, medical electronic scales use digital displays and sensors to provide precise weight measurements, often featuring advanced functionalities such as data storage and connectivity. Both types are essential for accurate patient assessment in healthcare settings.</p></p>
