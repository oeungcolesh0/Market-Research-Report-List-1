<p><p>Axial check valves are designed to prevent backflow in piping systems, and their market is categorized into three main types based on connection methods: </p><p>1. **Flanged End**: Featuring flanges for easy bolt-on installation, ideal for larger pipes. </p><p>2. **Threaded End**: Utilizing screw connections, these valves are suited for smaller installations and offer easy assembly and disassembly. </p><p>3. **Welding End**: These valves are directly welded to pipes, providing a seamless connection, enhancing durability and pressure integrity in demanding applications.</p></p>
