<p><p>The sports turf equipment market is divided into two main types: maneuvering and non-maneuvering equipment. Maneuvering equipment includes versatile machines like mowers and blowers designed for ease of operation and flexibility on the field, allowing for intricate movements around obstacles. In contrast, non-maneuvering equipment consists of stationary or less mobile machines, such as field rollers and aerators, which perform specific tasks with efficiency but require designated paths for operation, reducing operational flexibility. Both types play essential roles in maintaining sports fields.</p></p>
