<p><p>The feed antibiotics market comprises several categories, each serving distinct agricultural needs. Tetracyclines are broad-spectrum antibiotics effective against various infections in livestock. Penicillins target gram-positive bacteria, enhancing animal health. Sulfonamides exhibit bactericidal properties, often used in combination therapies. Macrolides are effective against respiratory pathogens in livestock. Aminoglycosides combat serious infections, particularly in poultry, while cephalosporins address bacterial infections with a wider spectrum. Together, these antibiotics contribute to improved animal growth, health, and productivity in the livestock industry.</p></p>
