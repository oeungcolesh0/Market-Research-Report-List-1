<p><p>Marking solutions can be categorized into three types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual systems require human intervention for every marking task, offering flexibility but less efficiency. Semi-automatic systems streamline the process by automating certain steps while still needing operator input, balancing productivity and control. Fully automatic systems optimize efficiency by completely eliminating manual involvement, enabling high-speed and high-volume marking with minimal human oversight, ideal for large-scale production environments.</p></p>
