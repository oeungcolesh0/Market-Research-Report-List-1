<p><p>The Liquid Thermal Interface Materials (TIM) market comprises three primary types: thermal adhesive fluids, thermal greases, and thermal liquid metals. Thermal adhesive fluids are utilized for bonding components while providing efficient heat transfer, making them ideal for applications requiring both adhesion and thermal performance. Thermal greases offer excellent thermal conductivity and fill microscopic gaps between surfaces, enhancing heat dissipation. Thermal liquid metals, known for their superior conductivity, are often used in high-performance applications where optimal thermal management is crucial.</p></p>
