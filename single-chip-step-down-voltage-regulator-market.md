<p><p>The single-chip step-down voltage regulator market is divided into two main types: AC step-down voltage regulators and DC step-down voltage regulators. AC step-down regulators convert higher AC voltage levels to lower AC voltages, typically for applications in lighting and appliances. In contrast, DC step-down regulators, or buck converters, reduce DC voltage from a higher level to a lower level, commonly used in battery-operated devices and consumer electronics. Both types enhance efficiency and power management in various applications.</p></p>
