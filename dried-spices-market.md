<p><p>The dried spices market can be categorized into three main types: powder, granule, and whole dried spices. Powder dried spices are finely ground, offering intense flavor and easy incorporation into recipes. Granule dried spices have a coarser texture, providing a balance between ease of use and flavor intensity. Whole dried spices maintain their natural form, preserving essential oils and fresher flavors, often requiring grinding before use. Each type caters to different culinary needs and preferences, affecting flavor release and application.</p></p>
