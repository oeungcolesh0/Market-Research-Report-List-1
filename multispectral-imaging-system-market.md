<p><p>The multispectral imaging system market can be categorized into two main types: single lens and multi-camera systems. Single lens systems utilize one camera to capture multiple spectral bands sequentially, offering a compact design suitable for smaller applications. Conversely, multi-camera systems employ several cameras to simultaneously capture various wavelengths, providing enhanced data acquisition speed and detail. Both approaches cater to diverse industries, including agriculture, environmental monitoring, and medical diagnostics, with each type presenting unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
