<p><p>The Rapid Antigen Test Kit market consists of various types, including Immunosorbent Assays that detect antigens utilizing antibodies on a solid substrate. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays use enzyme-linked antibodies for visualizing test results. Precipitation Reactions involve antibody-antigen complexes that form visible aggregates. Radioimmunoassays utilize radioactive isotopes for sensitive detection. Agglutination Reactions observe clumping of particles to identify antigens, while Complement Fixation tests measure immune responses by assessing complement consumption, providing diagnostic insights for infections and diseases.</p></p>
