<p><p>The motion capture tools market consists of several types, primarily divided into Optical 3D Motion Capture Systems and Inertial 3D Motion Capture Systems. Optical systems use cameras and markers to record an object's movement in a 3D space, providing high precision and detail, ideal for film and gaming. Inertial systems rely on sensors attached to the performer and capture motion through acceleration and rotation data, offering portability and ease of use. Other market segments may include hybrid systems and specialized applications in fields like biomechanics and virtual reality.</p></p>
