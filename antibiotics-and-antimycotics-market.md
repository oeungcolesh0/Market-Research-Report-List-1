<p><p>The antibiotics and antimycotics market is segmented into various types based on their chemical structure and action mechanism. **Penicillins** target bacterial cell walls, **cephalosporins** are broader-spectrum options, while **carbapenems** provide robust coverage against resistant bacteria. **Macrolides** inhibit protein synthesis, **aminoglycosides** are effective against serious infections, and **quinolones (fluoroquinolones)** disrupt DNA replication. **Sulfonamides** interfere with folic acid synthesis, and **tetracyclines** inhibit protein synthesis. The "other" market includes unique or emerging antimicrobials that don't fit conventional categories.</p></p>
