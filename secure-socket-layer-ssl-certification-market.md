<p><p>The Secure Socket Layer (SSL) certification market comprises three main types: Domain Validation (DV) Certification, Organization Validation (OV) Certification, and Extended Validation (EV) Certification. DV Certifications verify domain ownership and offer basic encryption, suitable for small websites. OV Certifications involve validating the organization’s identity, providing higher credibility for businesses. EV Certifications require thorough vetting of the organization, showcasing the highest level of trust and security, ideal for e-commerce and banking sites, thereby enhancing customer confidence.</p></p>
