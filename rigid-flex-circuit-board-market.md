<p><p>The rigid flex circuit board market is primarily categorized into two types: two-layer rigid-flex circuits and multi-layer rigid-flex circuits. Two-layer rigid-flex circuits consist of a limited number of conductive layers, offering flexibility and compactness for simpler applications. In contrast, multi-layer rigid-flex circuits incorporate numerous layers, enabling more complex designs and higher circuit density, making them suitable for advanced electronic devices. Both types are widely used in industries like aerospace, medical devices, and consumer electronics for their durability and functionality.</p></p>
