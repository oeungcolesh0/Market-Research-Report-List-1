<p><strong>The Organic Sport Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic</li><li>Hypotonic</li><li>Hypertonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic sport drink market comprises three main types: isotonic, hypotonic, and hypertonic. Isotonic drinks contain balanced concentrations of electrolytes and sugars, making them ideal for rapid hydration and energy replenishment during intense workouts. Hypotonic drinks have lower electrolyte levels, suitable for quick hydration after light exercise. In contrast, hypertonic drinks are rich in sugars and electrolytes, optimal for recovery after prolonged or strenuous activities. Each type caters to different hydration and energy needs in the sporting community.</p></p>
